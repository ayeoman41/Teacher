TECHNOLOGY ENGINEERING and DESIGN

This is an introductory course. Students are introduced to elements and principles of design, basic engineering, problem-solving and teaming. Students apply research and development skills and produce physical and virtural models. Activities are structured to integrate physical and social sciences, mathematics, English language arts and art.
